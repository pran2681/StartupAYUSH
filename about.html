About Startup AYUSH Portal

"स्टार्टअप आयुष पोर्टल् इत्यत्र स्वागतम्।"

Welcome to the Startup AYUSH portal. Our platform is driven by a passionate team committed to advancing the AYUSH ecosystem. With a deep understanding of the unique challenges and opportunities in the field, we are dedicated to creating a thriving environment for startups, investors, incubators, accelerators, government agencies, and the public to come together and collaborate.

Our journey began with the vision of establishing a global hub for the AYUSH community. We recognized the immense potential of AYUSH practices and products in promoting holistic well-being, and we wanted to provide a space where innovators and enthusiasts could connect, learn, and grow.

At Startup AYUSH, we believe that collaboration is the key to success. We have designed our portal to facilitate meaningful interactions, mentorship, and networking opportunities, enabling startups to flourish and reach new heights. We understand that entrepreneurship is a journey, and we are here to support you every step of the way.

Our commitment extends beyond virtual connections. We are constantly working to curate a wealth of resources and information guides to empower and equip all stakeholders in their entrepreneurial endeavors. Whether you're seeking funding, mentorship, knowledge, or simply looking to explore the AYUSH landscape, you'll find it here.

Join us on this exciting journey of innovation, growth, and transformation within the AYUSH ecosystem. Together, we can harness the power of ancient wisdom and modern entrepreneurship to create a healthier and more vibrant world. Thank you for being a part of the Startup AYUSH community.
